About Me

Hi, I am David Shontz, a student at the University of Central Florida. I am studying Kinesiology, and I took ENC 1102 this semester with Dr. Barnickle.

Before this class, I had never done a real semester-long research project. Writing portfolios were completely new to me. I was comfortable with the organizational side of school (folders, file names, deadlines) but nervous about revising older work. This semester pushed me to grow on both sides of that.

My research project this semester focused on Christian devotional websites like Our Daily Bread, YouVersion / Bible.com, and She Reads Truth, and how they use hope-based language and design to reach young adult readers across three decades. I chose this topic because Bible devotionals are part of my own everyday life, and I was curious about how the language and design choices on these sites have changed over time.

Outside of class, I follow the financial markets and trade, spend time at the gym, and stay involved with sports. After this class I am headed to physical therapy (PT) school, and on the side I am working toward managing a $1 million portfolio to trade.

Thanks for visiting my ePortfolio.
