Framing Statement

This is the capstone artifact of my semester. The final research paper pulls together everything from my annotated bibliography, literature review, data coding work, and practice claims into one CARS-model academic research article. The paper presents original primary research on Christian devotional websites, two argumentative claims, a counterclaim, and full appendices with the master coding table and the coded items list.

Why This Artifact Shows the Outcomes

This artifact shows Generating Inquiry because the paper follows my own research question through every section. It shows Multiple Ways of Writing because the paper includes a master coding table and four appendices with different modes of presentation. It shows Information Literacy because the paper uses only peer-reviewed scholarly sources found through the UCF library databases. It shows Research Genre Production because the paper follows the CARS model and has all required sections (Introduction, Literature Review, Methods, Results, Discussion, Conclusion, Works Cited, Appendices). It shows Contributing Knowledge because the paper presents original primary research findings and connects them back to the scholarly conversation. And it shows Revision because the paper went through multiple rounds, including a major revision after I noticed an early draft was too quote-heavy and did not sound like me.
